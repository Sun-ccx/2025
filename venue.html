Yamanashi University

Address: 4-4-37 Takeda, Kofu-shi, Yamanashi, 400-8510, Japan

Yamanashi University is a national university located in Kofu City, Yamanashi Prefecture, Japan. It offers a beautiful campus environment and modern conference facilities. The conference will be held at the main campus conference center.

Transportation Guide

From Tokyo

  • Take the Chuo Line Express from JR Tokyo Station
  • Get off at Kofu Station (approximately 2 hours)
  • Take a bus or taxi from Kofu Station to Yamanashi University (approximately 15 minutes)

From Haneda Airport

  • Take the airport bus directly to Kofu Station (approximately 2.5 hours)
  • Or transfer to the JR train to Kofu Station
  • Take a bus or taxi from Kofu Station to Yamanashi University

From Narita Airport

  • Take the JR Narita Express to Tokyo Station
  • Transfer to the Chuo Line to Kofu Station
  • Total travel time is approximately 4 hours

Local Attractions

Mount Fuji
A symbolic landmark of Japan, about 2 hours drive from Kofu, suitable for a day trip.

Kofu Castle Ruins
Historical site, a 15-minute walk from the venue, offering a panoramic view of Kofu City.

Yamanashi Winery
A famous wine-producing area in Japan, visitors can observe the winemaking process and taste wines.
